Privacy Policy for Minesweeper Battle Clash

Effective Date: September 29, 2025

MEETANSHI TECHNOLOGIES LLP (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how our mobile game Minesweeper Battle Clash operates and explains what information is (and is not) collected when you use the app.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information (such as name, email, phone number, contacts, or location).

  • No registration, sign-in, or account creation is required to play the game.
  • The game does not track your activity across other apps or websites.

Device Permissions

Minesweeper Battle Clash does not request or use any sensitive device permissions (such as access to camera, microphone, photos, storage, or location).

The game runs entirely without needing personal data access.

Gameplay Data

All progress (such as completed levels or scores) is stored locally on your device for your convenience.

This data is not uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

The game is safe and suitable for users of all ages.

  • We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.
  • Since no personal data is collected, there is no risk of misuse of children’s information.

Advertisements and Third-Party Services

The game does not include third-party advertisements.

We do not integrate analytics, trackers, or social media plugins that could collect user information.

Security

We are committed to ensuring your information remains secure. As no personal data is collected or transmitted, you can play Minesweeper Battle Clash with complete peace of mind.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be published with a revised “Effective Date” at the top of this page.
